Nintendo GameCube

Nintendo's magic cube. Learn how to take it to high definition and break free from optical discs.

2001 · 10 mods listed
🎨 Customize in 3D ⚖️ Compare setups

The Nintendo GameCube (2001) is an especially rich modding target: very high-quality digital HDMI output, optical drive emulators (ODE), open-source modchip and SD card loading. Depending on the model (DOL-001 with a Digital AV port, or DOL-101 without), the video solutions differ. Here are the go-to mods, sorted by category.

Video & HDMI

Take the GameCube to digital HDMI for a perfect image on modern displays. Internal kits tap into the digital video bus: DOL-001 models have a Digital AV port, while the DOL-101 (without it) needs digital-to-digital compatible kits.
