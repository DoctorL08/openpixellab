Gameboy Advance

The golden age of 32-bit handhelds. Learn how to turn your GBA into a powerful modern console.

2001 · 22 mods listed
🎨 Customize in 3D ⚖️ Compare setups

The Game Boy Advance (AGB-001, 2001) has just one real flaw: its original non-backlit screen, unreadable without a lamp. Fortunately, it's also one of the most modded consoles in the world. Laminated IPS and TFT screens, USB-C batteries, audio amps, RGB and clicky buttons: here are the go-to mods to wake up Nintendo's 32-bit handheld, sorted by category.

The must-have GBA mod: replacing the original reflective panel, unreadable without light, with a backlit IPS or TFT screen. Laminated versions remove dust and offer the best contrast; ITA kits install with no shell trimming.
