Gameboy Advance SP

The iconic clamshell design. The SP remains one of the most rewarding consoles to mod for play comfort.

2003 · 13 mods listed
🎨 Customize in 3D ⚖️ Compare setups

The Game Boy Advance SP (2003) comes in two variants: the AGS-001 (frontlit screen, often dark) and the AGS-101 (backlit screen, much brighter). Modern IPS screen mods fix both and deliver a vibrant image whatever the starting model. With its clamshell shell, built-in battery and compact electronics, the SP is an excellent comfort/modding compromise. Here are the go-to mods, sorted by category.
